Eat Clean Recipes

  • Client: The Cleanest Kitchen
  • Project: Website Design
  • Designer: Camila Duran
  • Document: Project Analysis

Site Intent

The goal of designing a page for clean recipes is intended for users to share, and obtain new recipes in two languages; Spanish and English. The website will contain 6 web pages which will contain clear content and will be easy to navigate. The purpose of the website to be simplistic is to have a high number of visitors. Visitors will have access to a limited number of recipes. Unlimited number of recipes will be provided once the user subscribes to the website. The website will provide recipes in English and Spanish to expand the number of users as well.

Reduced content for the website will make it simple and more accessible for the subscriber to navigate each page, making it simple to share recipes and obtain recipes from other users. The limitation of recipes daily will drive the user to subscribe, allowing them to an unlimited number of recipes. The simplistic website will generate a high number of users due to the easy way of navigation and accessing information and the fact that it contains recipes in Spanish and English would create a more broad number of users.

Competitive Analysis

When searching keywords “Eating Clean Recipes” only a few websites stood up that were only for clean eating. The top three are:

www.cookinglight.com: Global rank is 6,533. Rank in the United States is 1,457. According to www.alexa.com, the website is mainly visited by female users and the majority of the visitors are from the United Sates (74.3% of the users).

www.pinterest.com: Pinterest is not really a website solemnly for Eating Clean Recipes. To be able to access the recipes a subscriber must first subscriber to Pinterest. The global rank for this website is 26 and the rank in the United States is 12.

www.cleaneatingmag.com: Global rank is 103,331. Rank in the United States is 29,374. According to www.alexa.com, the website is mainly visited by female users and the majority of the visitors are from the United States.

Target Audience:

Our targeted audience is mainly residential. Individuals who wish to lose weight in a healthy way as well as individuals who are physically fit and just wish to add healthy and clean eating habits to an already active live style. This includes both genders, male and female of any age (adult age depending on the country where the website is being accessed from). In addition to that, the website will provide the option of recipes in Spanish to expand the range of users and to be able to reach countries in Central America as well as South America (some countries in Europe as well).
